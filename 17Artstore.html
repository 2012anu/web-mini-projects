Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere

by Jean-Auguste-Dominique Ingres

The port Mademoiselle Caroline Riviere was painted 1806 by the French Neoclassical artist Jean Auguste Domninique Ingres, and today hangs in louvre. It is the of three portraits of the Riviere family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimension: 100*70 cm
Home: Musee du Louvre, Paris
